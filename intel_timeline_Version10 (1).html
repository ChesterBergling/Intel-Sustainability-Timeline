Intel Timeline

1968 — Intel Founded

Intel was founded in 1968, marking the beginning of the modern semiconductor industry.

1971 — First Microprocessor

Intel introduced the world’s first commercial microprocessor, revolutionizing computing.

1978 — Intel 8086

The Intel 8086 microprocessor launched, laying the foundation for the x86 architecture.

1985 — Intel Innovation

Intel continued to push forward with powerful processors, shaping the future of PCs.

1993 — Pentium Processor

Intel launched the Pentium processor, bringing superscalar architecture to mainstream computing.

2006 — Core Architecture

Intel introduced Core architecture, revolutionizing multi-core processor design and energy efficiency.

2017 — Advanced Manufacturing

Intel advanced to 10nm process technology, pushing the boundaries of semiconductor manufacturing.

2023 — AI & Modern Computing

Intel continues innovation with AI acceleration and advanced processor architectures for modern computing needs.
